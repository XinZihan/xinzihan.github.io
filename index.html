Hello, I'm Zihan

Zihan Xin is a graduate student in Library and Information Science at Western University, with an academic background in history and journalism from the University of Toronto. His interests lie at the intersection of information management, digital media, and public communication. He has developed experience in web content creation, digitization workflows, and outreach through both academic projects and professional roles, including his work as a marketing intern supporting digital strategy and content development. Zihan’s work reflects a curiosity about how information is organized, accessed, and communicated in contemporary digital environments. He is particularly interested in the role of libraries, media, and technology in shaping public knowledge and accessibility.
